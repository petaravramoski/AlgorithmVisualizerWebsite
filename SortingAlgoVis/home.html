Welcome to the Sorting Algorithm Visualizer, a project created by Petar Avramoski for my portfolio. This interactive web application is designed to showcase the world of sorting algorithms through visual representations and hands-on exploration.

At its core, the Sorting Algorithm Visualizer offers a dynamic platform for users to interact with various sorting algorithms, providing a unique opportunity to witness the inner workings of these algorithms in real-time. Whether you're a computer science enthusiast, a student, or simply curious about the mechanics of sorting, this project aims to make complex concepts more accessible and engaging. Through this visualizer, you can not only gain insights into sorting algorithms but also, adjust sorting speeds, and compare the efficiency of different algorithms.
